The Core Issue
Healthcare is Fragmented & Slow
⏳

Delayed Diagnostics

Patients wait days for radiology reviews. In critical conditions like brain tumors, every hour lost impacts survival rates.

🧩

Siloed Data

Scans, consultations, and mental health tracking happen on separate, disconnected platforms. There is no unified patient view.

🧠

Mental Health Stigma

Lack of accessible, private preliminary mental health screening leaves conditions like depression and anxiety severely under-diagnosed.

Diagnostics

  • Clinical Report Generation: Uses Groq LLaMA-3.3-70b to write structured, radiologist-style findings automatically.
