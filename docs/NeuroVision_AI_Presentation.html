1. The Vision & The "Why"
Building a Hospital in the Cloud

The Broken Reality

  • ✖ Siloed Systems: Booking, diagnostics, and therapy happen entirely disconnected from one another.
  • ✖ Delayed Answers: Getting a specialist radiologist to review an X-ray in rural areas takes days.
  • ✖ The Stigma: Mental health issues remain unaddressed due to high costs and social barriers.

Our Solution

  • ✔ Unified Access: Democratizing access to clinical-grade intelligence without spatial or economic boundaries.
  • ✔ Instant Diagnostics: 3-second autonomous AI scan analysis.
  • ✔ Empathetic Grounding: Conversational AI and Voice Therapy bridging the gap to traditional care.

05 / 15
Subsystem 01
CustomNet121 (Chest X-Ray)
  • Architecture: A heavily fine-tuned, custom implementation of DenseNet121.
  • Code Implementation: Coded in finetune_chest_model.py. We mapped specific pathologies via ImageDataGenerator and applied a custom Cosine Decay learning rate schedule using Keras.
  • Dataset: Trained on the raw Kaggle NIH ChestX-ray8 Dataset (112,120 labeled images).
  • Performance: Over 10 epochs (batch size 8), loss dropped by 18% down to 0.153.
  • Log File: Fully documented and proven locally in chest_finetune_history.csv.
Final Validation Accuracy 94.4%
11-Class Diagnosis
06 / 15
Subsystem 02
InceptionV3 (Brain Tumor MRI)
  • Architecture: InceptionV3 layered with custom fully-connected classification heads.
  • Code Implementation: Engineered end-to-end in our raw Jupyter notebook braintTumor.ipynb, preprocessing all raw array images natively.
  • Dataset: The expansive Brain Tumor MRI Dataset consisting of 7,023 images.
  • Classification Array: 4 distinct classes: Glioma, Meningioma, Pituitary tumor, and no tumor.
Clinical Screening Standard 98.5%
Neurology Specialization
07 / 15
Subsystems 03 & 04
Transformers & Validation

🦴 MedSigLIP (Orthopedics)

Architecture: HuggingFace Vision-Language Transformer.

Approach: By using image-text pairing, this model leverages Zero-Shot Classification. We dynamically compare any orthopedic bone scan against 20+ text pathology labels without explicitly fine-tuning image classifiers for all 20 specific bone shapes.

📋 CheXpert DenseNet

Dataset: Stanford's CheXpert (224,316 images).

Approach: Validates outputs alongside CustomNet121 specifically to map 14 localized chest pathologies (like Edema vs Atelectasis) providing the multi-variable probabilities seen natively on our frontend diagnostic UI bars.

08 / 15
Subsystem 05
NLP & PDF Engineering
  • Inference Engine: Instead of the standard OpenAI API, we adopted the revolutionary LPU (Language Processing Unit) acceleration of Groq (LLaMA-3.3-70b). It translates PyTorch numeric arrays into 1,500-token empathetic PDF clinician reports.
  • Local Custom Infrastructure: We didn't *just* rely on an API. We wrote our own internal NLP training pipeline directly in fine_tune_llm.py.
  • MedGemma Implementation: Utilizing the Hugging Face AutoModelForCausalLM, we constructed the MedicalLLMFineTuner class to ingest JSON diagnostic corpora and apply PyTorch Data Collators.
"Proving our architecture can operate entirely on localized, fine-tuned google/medgemma-27b-it edge instances for specialized contingencies when internet API connectivity drops."

4. The Product (Part 1/2)
9 Platform Modules

1. NeuroVision Diagnostics

The core hub. Upload files, trigger E2E inference, visualize finding bars, generate the AI text report, and download the clinical ReportLab PDF.

2. MedBot AI Chat

A 24/7 symptom checker floating natively on the patient dashboard. Acts as an empathetic, always-on triage nurse.

3. Aria (Voice Therapist)

Utilizing the native Web Speech API and Groq responses, users can verbally speak into their microphones for a conversational mental-health grounding session without typing.

4. Mental Health Planner

Automatically tests users with clinically recognized scales: PHQ-9 for depression severity and GAD-7 for anxiety, outputting score gauges and actionable routines.

4. The Product (Part 2/2)
9 Platform Modules

5. Cognitive Tracking Games

Tracks neurological baselines through memory-pattern games mimicking the MMSE (Mini-Mental State Exam) and execution speed tracing TMT tests.
