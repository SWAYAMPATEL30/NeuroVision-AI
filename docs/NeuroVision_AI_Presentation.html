The Challenge
Why does healthcare need AI now?
⏳

Long Wait Times

Radiology reports take days. AI can analyse a scan in under 3 seconds — giving patients faster, earlier answers.

💸

Prohibitive Costs

Specialist consultation fees can be unaffordable. AI pre-screening allows people to understand risk before expensive visits.

🗺️

Geographic Barriers

Rural populations lack access to specialists. A cloud-backed AI platform removes geography from the equation entirely.

🧠

Mental Health Neglect

Stigma and inaccessibility leave mental health under-diagnosed. PHQ-9, GAD-7 & a 24/7 AI therapist changes this.

💡 NeuroVision AI solves all four with a single, integrated platform — diagnostics, booking, therapy, and education in one place.

Intelligence Layer
5-Model AI Ensemble
ModelSpecialtyArchitectureDatasetPerformance

InceptionV3 🧠 Brain MRI InceptionV3 Brain Tumor MRI (7,023 images) 98%+ acc
MedSigLIP 🦴 Bone / General Vision-Language Transformer HuggingFace medical pairs Zero-Shot · 20+ labels
CheXpert DenseNet 🫁 Chest (14-label) DenseNet121 Stanford CheXpert (224K images) 14 Pathologies
Groq LLaMA-3.3-70b 📝 Clinical Text NLP Large Language Model General + Medical corpus <2s · 1500 token reports
Routing Logic:
Chest → CustomNet121 primary
Bone → MedSigLIP zero-shot
Brain → InceptionV3 4-class
Text → Groq NLP
General → Full ensemble
Output per analysis:
✅ Primary diagnosis + confidence score
✅ Full probability distribution
✅ Groq-generated clinical report
✅ Downloadable PDF (ReportLab)
✅ Auto-saved to Supabase DB

Features
9 Platform Modules
🔬

NeuroVision Diagnostics

Upload X-ray / MRI. AI analyses in <3s. Download PDF clinical report with FINDINGS, IMPRESSION, RECOMMENDATIONS.

🤖

MedBot AI Chat

24/7 Groq llama-3.3-70b health assistant. Symptom analysis, medical Q&A, always recommends doctor follow-up.

🎙️

Voice Therapist (Aria)

Real-time voice-based mental health companion. Web Speech API + Groq. Chrome/Edge supported.

📅

Doctor Booking

Live Leaflet map. Filter by specialty. Book online (video) or offline (in-person). AI pre-screening attachment.

🧠

Mental Health Planner

PHQ-9 depression screening. GAD-7 anxiety test. MMSE cognitive baseline. AI-generated wellness plans.

🎮

Cognitive Games

3 interactive neurological assessments: memory (MMSE), speed (TMT), verbal recall (MoCA).

👨‍👩‍👧

Family Connect

Create shareable health profiles. Caregiver access via UUID link. Up to 10 family members.

🌐

VR Connect

FrameVR-powered immersive 3D room for group health consultations. Camera + mic support.

📚

Resource Hub

Curated medical articles, videos & tools. Filter by category, difficulty. Mental health to cardiology.
